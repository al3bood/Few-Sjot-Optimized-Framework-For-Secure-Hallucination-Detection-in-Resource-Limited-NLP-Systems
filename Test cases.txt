Knowledge (K)

Apollo 11 was the first crewed mission to land on the Moon.

The mission was launched by NASA on July 16, 1969.

The spacecraft carried Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin, and Michael Collins.

After travelling for several days, Apollo 11 landed on the Moon on July 20, 1969.

Neil Armstrong became the first human to walk on the lunar surface.

Many other details about mission preparation, rocket specifications,
communication systems, training procedures, recovery operations,
historical background, and public reactions are also documented.

Question (Q)

On what date did Apollo 11 land on the Moon?

Right Answer (RA)

July 20, 1969
